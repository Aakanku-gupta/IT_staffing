OUR CAPABILITIES

STAFF AUGMENTATION


Staff augmentation is a business strategy that involves hiring additional staff on a temporary basis to supplement an existing workforce. The term "staff augmentation" refers to the process of filling gaps in a company's workforce by adding temporary employees who have specific skills or expertise. This strategy is often used by companies to handle peak workloads, to complete specific projects, or to fill gaps in specialized skills that are not available within the existing workforce.


CONTRACT-TO-HIRE


Contract-to-hire is a type of employment agreement where a company hires an employee on a temporary or contract basis, with the intention of offering them a permanent position after a specified period of time. During the contract period, the employee works for the company on a temporary basis, typically for a few months to a year.The contract-to-hire arrangement gives both the employer and the employee an opportunity to evaluate each other's suitability for a long-term employment relationship.


DIRECT HIRE & PERMANENT PLACEMENT


Direct hire generally refers to the process of hiring an employee directly into a permanent, full-time position, without any intermediary contracts or temporary assignments. In this case, the employer takes responsibility for all aspects of the hiring process, including sourcing candidates, conducting interviews, and making the final hiring decision. Direct hire is often used for positions that require specific skills or experience, or that are critical to the company's core operations.


PROJECT TEAM STAFFING


Project team staffing refers to the process of selecting and hiring employees to work on a specific project or set of projects. The project team may be made up of a variety of professionals, including project managers, technical specialists, designers, developers, and other specialists with skills and expertise relevant to the project's goals.
